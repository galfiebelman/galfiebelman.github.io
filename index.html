Gal Fiebelman
 

I am a Computer Science PhD Candidate at The Hebrew University of Jerusalem, under the supervision of Dr. Sagie Benaim. I am interested in computer graphics, computer vision and machine learning. Specifically, my research focuses on high-level semantic understanding and manipulation of 3D and 4D representations.

I received my MSc from Tel Aviv University in Computer Science under the supervision of Dr. Hadar Averbuch-Elor and my BSc from The Hebrew University of Jerusalem in Mathematics and Computer Science. I also spent a few years in the industry developing Computer Vision algorithms.
